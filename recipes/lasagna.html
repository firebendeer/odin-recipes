Chicken Taco Lasagna Recipe

Description and Nutrition Facts

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna.

Prep time: 20 min Cook: 1 hour 15 min Servings: 8

Per Serving:

  • 271 calories
  • 19.9 g protein
  • 21.6 g carbohydrates
  • 11.4g fat
  • 56.7 mg cholesterol
  • 904.5 mg sodium

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 4 bonelss skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4 inch pieces
  • half cup lime juice
  • 2 packets (1 ounce) taco seasoning mix
  • 1 and a half cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and pepper, according to personal taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice and taco seasoning in a bowl. Stir to make sure the chicken is evenly covered.
  2. Cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm, about 15 min.
  3. Mix half a cup of mozzarella cheese, sour cream and salsa in a bowl.
  4. Put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture at the bottom of a 9x9 inch baking dish. Layer noodles and chicken on top. Cover with remaining 1 cup mozarella chese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted, about 1 hours.
