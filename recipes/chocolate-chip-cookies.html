Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description and Nutrition Facts

Crisp edges, chewy middles

Prep time: 20 min Cook time: 10 min Servings: 24 pieces

Per Serving:

  • 298 calories
  • 3.6 g protein
  • 38.9 g carbohydrates
  • 15.6 g fat
  • 35.8 mg cholesterol
  • 165.8 mg sodium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups baking flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the buter, white sugar and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with a bit of salt.
  3. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and walnuts. Drop by large spoonfuls
